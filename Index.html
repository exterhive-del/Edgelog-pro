Your trade data stays on your device. We never see it, collect it, or sell it. The only data that leaves your device is when you use AI features — your trade stats are sent to Groq's API to generate a response.

EdgeLog Pro does not collect, store, or transmit your trading data. All records are stored in your browser's localStorage. We have no access to this data.
